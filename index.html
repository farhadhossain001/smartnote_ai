About SmartNote AI

SmartNote AI revolutionizes the way you capture and organize your thoughts. Our intelligent note-taking app combines the power of artificial intelligence with intuitive design to help you be more productive than ever before.

Whether you're a student, professional, or creative thinker, SmartNote AI adapts to your workflow and enhances your note-taking experience with features like automatic summarization, voice transcription, and smart organization.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the app free?

SmartNote AI offers a free version with all features. Premium features like advanced AI summarization and rewrites are available in this version.

Does it work offline?

Yes! You can create, edit, and view your notes completely offline. AI features require an internet connection, but your notes are always accessible.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption for all your notes and follow industry-standard security practices. Your data is never shared with third parties.

Can I sync across devices?

No, the notes only saved locally on your device. So there is no option to sync across devices.
